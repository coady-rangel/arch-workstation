# Engineering Maxims

---

## Purpose

Engineering maxims are concise statements that capture the values and lessons learned throughout the life of this project.

Unlike implementation details, these ideas are intended to remain useful across projects and over time.

When faced with a difficult decision, revisit these maxims before choosing a path.

---

# Engineering Maxims

> **Invest in systems that reduce the cost of future work.**

---

> **Designing a system is the hard part. Putting the plan to action is easier and fun.**

---

> **We're not just building software. We're building a repeatable way of building software.**

---

> **A project is a living system, not a finished artifact.**

---

> **The foundation should make every future change easier.**

---

> **Code explains how. Documentation explains why.**

---

> **Small improvements compound into extraordinary systems.**

---

## Living Document

This document is intentionally incomplete.

As new lessons are learned, new maxims should be added.

The goal is not to collect clever quotes.

The goal is to preserve the engineering mindset that shaped this project.
